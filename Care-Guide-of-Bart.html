Bart's Care Guide

Food & Water

  • Bart eats wet dog food twice a day, 150g per meal. If he’s had a particularly active day with lots of playtime, feel free to increase the portion slightly based on his appetite.
  • Always ensure that his water bowl is filled—fresh water should be available to him at all times. (Although I'm also forget sometimes.)
  • When in doubt about whether Bart can eat something, just ask ChatGPT.

Walk Schedule

  • Weekdays: No walks are needed during the workdays. If you take him to office.
  • Weekends: Bart should be walked at 9:00 a.m. and 7:00 p.m. If he drinks a lot of water in the evening, an additional short walk around 10:00–11:00 p.m. might be necessary.
  • After each walk, please wipe Bart’s feet and belly to keep him clean.

Sleeping Habits

At night, Bart typically starts off sleeping on the bed, but he often jumps down later to sleep on his own. By morning, he usually wants to return to the bed. If he has trouble jumping up, feel free to lift him back onto the bed.

Important Notes

  • Recall Warning: Bart may not return when called if there are other dogs around. Before letting him off the leash, make sure you have a snack in your pocket to help call him back when needed.
  • When Left Alone: If Bart is home alone, he might dig through the trash, chew tissues, or bite socks. He doesn’t open drawers, but to be safe, keep any valuable or fragile items out of reach before leaving him alone.
  • Plants: Bart sometimes digs in potted soil and may chew on plant leaves. Please make sure to keep any toxic plants out of his reach.
